ABOUT

JOHN J. KIM

John J. Kim is a designer by day, animator by night. Born and raised in Flushing, NY, he possesses both great enthusiasm and deep interest in the artistic mediums. He has spent many years honing and refining his craftsmanship through comic books and various animated short series.

His work has an endless range, stretching from original cartoon characters to animating digital persons on software. As his involvement in animation continues to grow, he soughts after his ultimate in performing animation through computer graphics while residing in New York.
